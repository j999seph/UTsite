# UTsite Project Brief

## Brand direction
Unique Trades is a discreet, globally connected, relationship-driven trade and opportunity site.

## Positioning
The site should feel like a private international business network, not a public trading directory.

## Audience
- Thoughtful decision makers
- Relationship-driven entrepreneurs
- Long-term players
- Serious business people
- Globally minded operators

## Discouraged audience
- Price shoppers
- Transactional buyers
- Mass RFQ senders
- Low-commitment inquiries
- Quick-deal seekers

## Tone keywords
Discretion, capability, global reach, adaptability, confidence, problem solving, relationship building.

## Avoid
- Overly technical language
- Loud marketing claims
- Snobby or pretentious tone
- Over-explaining the scope
- Product/service catalog language

## Homepage phrasing references
- "Unique Trades"
- "Beyond Conventional Trade"
- "Not every opportunity fits inside conventional boundaries."
- "Bring us your challenge..."

## Layout direction
- One-page site
- Anchored navigation
- Floating jump buttons acceptable
- Clean layout
- Strategic whitespace
- Strong CTA in contact area

## Palette reference
- Deep navy
- Prussian blue
- Shadow grey
- Glaucous
- Deep space blue

## Notes
Keep this document updated as client feedback arrives.
